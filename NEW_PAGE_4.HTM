MONGOLIAN DEATH WORM.

 

 

DESCRIPTION - 

Locally known as ALLGHOI KHORKHOI, the mongolian death worm, it is said to be bright red snakelike animal two to four feet long and sprays it's victims with venom or acid. which could kill a man or even a camel.                                                                         

SIGHTINGS -

No Westerners have ever seen it, but locals and nomads fear it. The mear mention of it's name is bad luck.                                                                                            There are many stories of the worm killing people, but this has yet to be proved.

DIET -

Unknown.

LOCATION -

Gobi desert, Mongolia
