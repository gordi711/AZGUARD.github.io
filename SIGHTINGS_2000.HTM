SIGHTINGS 2000

 

APRIL 29 2000

LONDON

TWO OBJECTS WERE SEEN OVER THE CITY FOR 15 MINUTES. THE UFO'S WERE DESCRIBED AS TWO SPHERE LIKE OBJECTS MOVING IN SINK, SIMILAR TO A DUMBBELL DOING AEROBATIC MANEUVERS.

 

JULY 10 2000

NORTH YORKSHIRE

TWO MOTORISTS SPOTTED A LARGE BLUE ORB THAT STAYED STATIONARY FOR AT LEAST 20 MINUTES, THEN MOVED OFF AT INCREDIBLE SPEED. THE TWO WITNESSES WERE DISTRESSED BY THE INCIDENT.

 

JULY 2000

UK

A PARTY OF FRIENDS SPOTTED A LARGE BLACK SAUCER SHAPED OBJECT OVER BENNINGTON. DESCRIBED AS FAST MOVING WITH MULTIPLE SEARCH LIGHTS AND RED LIGHTS AROUND THE RIM.
