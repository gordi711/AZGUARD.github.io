SIGHTINGS 2001

JULY 2001

BIRMINGHAM

ON THE MORNING OF THE JULY 9 2001, A WOMAN WAS OBSERVING A PLANE WITH BINOCULARS WHEN HER ATTENTION WAS DRAWN TO A WHITE SPHERICAL OBJECT MOVING AT A 90 DEGREE ANGLE TO THE PLANE. IT WAS BEING TRACKED WHEN ANOTHER SPHERICAL OBJECT RIGHT BEHIND IT, THEY STAYED IN FOCUS FOR ANOTHER 20 SECONDS THEN VANISHED.

JUNE 2001

GLASGOW

A PHOTOGRAPHER UNKNOWINGLY CAPTURED A SAUCER-SHAPED OBJECT, ONLY WHEN EXAMINING THE PHOTOGRAPHS HE DISCOVERED THE DARK SILHOUETTE OF THE UFO ON A BACKGROUND OF CLOUD OVER THE CITY. THE PHOTOGRAPHS HAVE YET TO BE AUTHENTICATED, BUT THE M.O.D ARE SAID TO BE INTERESTED.

MARCH 2001

WOOTON

A MOTORIST SPOTTED A V-SHAPED CRAFT HOVERING IN THE SKY IN THE AREA OF WOOTON BARRETT THE OBJECT SEEMED TO ROTATE EVERY 10 SECONDS OR SO UNTIL IT VANISHED 30 MINUTES LATER.
