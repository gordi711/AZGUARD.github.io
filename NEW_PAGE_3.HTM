ELCAPUCARA.

THE GOAT SUCKER

 

DESCRIPTION - ACROSS BETWEEN A KANGAROO AND AN ALIEN GRAY, AROUND 4 FEET TALL, LARGE RED EYES, FANGS, CLAWED ARMS AND BAT LIKE WINGS, BUT WITH LARGE MUSCULAR LEGS. 

 

SIGHTINGS - THE FIRST SIGHTINGS WERE IN PUERTO RICO A COMMONWEALTH OF THE UNITED STATES, WITH ATTACKS ON LIVESTOCK.

THEN THE SIGHTINGS SPREAD MEXICO AND THE SOUTHERN UNITED STATES IE MIAMI AND TAMPA, BUT THESE ATTACKS WERE NOT ONLY ON LIVESTOCK AND PETS BUT PEOPLE TOO. 

 

DIET - MAINLY LIVESTOCK, CHICKENS, GOATS AND SOME PETS, IT HAS ATTACKED PEOPLE BUT WITH NO SUCCESS. THE BODY'S OF IT'S VICTIMS HAVE SMALL WOUNDS MAINLY ON THE NECK OR BACK AND ARE DRAINED OF BLOOD ALTHOUGH SOME ANIMALS ARE LEFT ALIVE IN A ZOMBIE LIKE STATE, BUT DIE A FEW HOURS LATER.

 

LOCATION - PUERTO RICO AND SOUTHERN UNITED STATES.
